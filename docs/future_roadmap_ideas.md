# TigerResearchBuddy: Future Roadmap & Advanced Ideas

This document stores advanced concepts and future features planned for the Collaboration Platform.

## 🚀 Advanced Intelligence & Collaboration

### 1. AI Synergy Reports
**Concept**: Beyond simple matching, generate actionable validation for *why* a collaboration makes sense.
- **Function**: When a match is found (e.g., Biology + Computer Science), use an LLM to generate a 1-page "Joint Proposal Starter".
- **Content**: Potential grant titles, combined methodology, and shared goals.

### 2. Resource & Equipment Matching
**Concept**: Match departments not just by ideas, but by physical/digital assets.
- **Example**: "Dept A has a High-Performance Cluster" <-> "Dept B has a large dataset needing training".
- **Implementation**: A "Resource Inventory" metadata tag for each department.

### 3. Cross-Pollination Agent
**Concept**: An autonomous background agent.
- **Behavior**: It "reads" recent papers from all departments.
- **Action**: It proactively posts comments or emails researchers: *"I noticed you're working on X. Did you know Dr. Smith in History is studying the historical context of X?"*

### 4. "The Prism" - 3D Knowledge Graph
**Concept**: Upgrade the 2D collaboration graph to a fully interactive 3D WebGL experience.
- **Visuals**: A rotating galaxy of research nodes.
- **Interaction**: profound zooming and filtering to explore the entire university's research output.

### 5. Automated Grant Scouting
**Concept**: The AI monitors Grants.gov or NSF.gov.
- **Action**: When a grant matches a *combination* of two departments' strengths, it notifies both: *"If you work together, you have a high chance of winning this grant."*
